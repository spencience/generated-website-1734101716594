Our Mission

Founded in 2018, the BRELOOM #286 Research Institute is dedicated to exploring the fascinating intersections between Pokémon #286 (Breloom), medical classification systems, and numerical patterns in our world.

Our groundbreaking research, led by Dr. Luigi Mangione, has uncovered remarkable connections between these seemingly unrelated fields, leading to new insights in both healthcare and Pokémon studies.
